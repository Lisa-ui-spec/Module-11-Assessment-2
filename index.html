The world's best reviewed restaurants


Table by Verjas

Paris London

Table provides truly creative food that also manages to be delicious. Chef Bruno plays with flavors and concepts in a way that brings the patron to the origin. Well know for their best desserts.

St Lawarence Market

Toronto

"Nestled under open skies, this outdoor café invites you to savor coffee in the fresh air, surrounded by the sights and sounds of the city.Their signature coffee is] crafted with locally roasted beans, giving each cup a bold and unique flavor that stands out. Every sip delivers a rich, complex blend that’s perfected to bring out the best of each bean—an experience that can only be matched by the serene setting itself.

Belcanto Burgers

Tokyo

"This outdoor burger stall is a must-visit for anyone craving a bold, unforgettable bite. Known for their massive, stacked burgers, they take pride in serving juicy, flavorful patties that are seasoned to perfection and grilled right in front of you. Topped with fresh, generous ingredients and served on a warm, toasted bun, each burger is an explosion of flavor. But what makes them the number one choice? It’s their commitment to quality and creativity—their signature sauces and unique topping combinations set their burgers apart, making every bite a new experience that keeps customers coming back for more."

Queen Victoria Market

Morocco

"Set against the soothing backdrop of ocean waves, this beachside restaurant brings a unique twist to traditional Massaman curry. Known for their authentic take on the classic dish, they blend tender cuts of meat with a rich, aromatic curry sauce infused with locally sourced spices and coconut milk for a creamy depth of flavor. What sets them apart is their commitment to freshness—their Massaman curry is slow-cooked to perfection , allowing the flavors to meld beautifully, and it’s always served with a view of the water that enhances the experience. Dining here isn’t just about great food; it’s a feast for all the senses, leaving you with the warmth of Thai cuisine and the beauty of the beach."

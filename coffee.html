Reviewed Coffees


Cubano Coffee

Café de la Isla , India

Café de la Isla is a vibrant spot that captures the spirit of Cuban culture, famous for its exceptional café cubano. Each cup is a bold and aromatic espresso shot, expertly brewed with finely ground, dark-roasted coffee beans, creating a rich flavor profile that's both sweet and intense. The baristas at Café de la Isla skillfully whip up a thick layer of sweet espuma, or foam, that crowns the espresso, enhancing its unique taste. Served in small, inviting cups, this café cubano is a delightful experience that embodies the warmth and passion of Cuba, making it a must-try for coffee enthusiasts seeking the best in the world.

Frappe Coffee

Chill & Brew cAFE, Tokyo

Chill & Brew is a beloved café renowned for its exceptional frappe coffee, offering a refreshing twist on this classic drink. Each frappe is expertly blended to create a creamy, frothy delight that perfectly balances rich coffee flavor with just the right amount of sweetness. Made with premium coffee beans and served over ice, it’s toppe with a generous dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon for an extra touch of indulgence. At Chill & Brew, the frappe coffee is not just a drink; it’s an experience that keeps customers coming back for the best in the world, perfect for any time of day.

Expresso

Cafe Aroma, Greece

Cafe Aroma is a coffee lover’s paradise, celebrated for its exquisite espresso that embodies the true essence of Italian coffee culture. Each shot is meticulously crafted using carefully selected, freshly roasted beans, resulting in a rich, bold flavor profile with a velvety crema on top. The skilled baristas at Cafe Aroma take pride in their craft, ensuring that every espresso is brewed to perfection, highlighting notes of chocolate, caramel, and subtle fruitiness. Whether enjoyed solo or as part of a delightful coffee experience, Cafe Aroma’s espresso is considered the best in the world, inviting coffee enthusiasts to savor every sip..

Latte Coffee

Artisan Brews cafe , Canada

"Artisan Brews is a cozy café celebrated for its exceptional lattes, where each cup is a work of art crafted with precision and care. Using freshly roasted, high-quality beans, the skilled baristas expertly pull rich, velvety shots of espresso, then steam creamy milk to perfection, creating a harmonious blend that’s both smooth and invigorating. Topped with delicate latte art that showcases their creativity, every sip offers a balanced sweetness and deep flavor that delights the senses. At Artisan Brews, the latte isn’t just a beverage; it’s an experience that has earned a reputation as the best in the world, inviting coffee lovers to indulge in the perfect pick-me-up.

Americano Coffee

Brewed Awekening restaurant , Belgium

Brewed Awakening is a coffee lover’s haven, famous for its exceptional Americano that redefines this classic favorite. Each cup is crafted with precision, starting with a robust shot of freshly brewed espresso, which is then perfectly diluted with hot water to create a rich, bold flavor profile. The skilled baristas select the finest beans, ensuring that every sip is full of depth and complexity, with notes of chocolate and caramel that dance on the palate. Served in a warm, inviting atmosphere, the Americano at Brewed Awakening is not just a drink; it’s an experience that has garnered a loyal following, making it the best in the world for those seeking a true coffee moment.

Mocha Coffee

Cocoa & Coffee, Japan

Cocoa & Coffee is a delightful café celebrated for its luxurious mocha, a perfect fusion of rich espresso and velvety chocolate. Each cup begins with a perfectly pulled shot of espresso, blended with high-quality, melted dark chocolate to create a decadent base. Steamed milk is then artfully poured in, resulting in a creamy texture that balances the boldness of the coffee and the sweetness of the chocolate. Topped with a generous swirl of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa, this mocha is a true indulgence for chocolate lovers. At Cocoa & Coffee, every sip is a blissful escape, making it a must-try for anyone seeking the best mocha in the world.

Reviewed Meals


Kebab

Pitogyros Oia Street stall, Greece

Nestled in the heart of the city, Pitogyros has earned a global reputation for crafting the world’s most succulent, flavor-packed kebabs. Each skewer is a masterpiece, seasoned with a blend of spices that have been perfected over generations. From tender, melt-in-your-mouth lamb to smoky, char-grilled chicken, every bite captures the essence of Middle Eastern culinary tradition with a contemporary twist. The chefs use only the freshest ingredients and high-quality meats, marinating each cut to enhance its natural flavors. Whether you’re a kebab connoisseur or new to the experience, Pitogyros Oia promises an unforgettable culinary journey with every skewer, making it a must-visit for lovers of authentic, world-class kebabs.

Masala

Hotel Savanna Bhavan, Tokyo

"Savana Bhavan is a paradise for masala lovers, offering an experience that brings the true heart of Indian spices to your plate. Known for its unrivaled, aromatic masalas, the restaurant blends spices in-house, creating rich, vibrant flavors that elevate each dish to new heights. From their signature masala curries that simmer to perfection to their expertly spiced biryanis and roasted meats, every bite delivers an explosion of flavors and warmth. The chefs at Savanna Baven have honed their craft over decades, carefully balancing each spice to honor traditional recipes while infusing a modern touch. With each mouthful, you’ll taste why this restaurant is celebrated globally as a masala masterpiece.

Briyani

Paradise restaurant , India

Paradise restaurant is a culinary gem that has perfected the art of biryani, delivering a dish that’s truly unforgettable. Known worldwide for its fragrant, flavorful biryani, the restaurant uses only the finest long-grain basmati rice, tender marinated meats, and a proprietary blend of spices that’s been passed down through generations. Each serving arrives steaming, with saffron-kissed rice and juicy, well-seasoned pieces of chicken or lamb nestled within, infused with flavors that capture the essence of India’s culinary soul. Cooked traditionally in clay pots and sealed for maximum aroma, every bite at Biryani Bliss is a symphony of spices, texture, and tradition, making it a paradise for biryani lovers everywhere.

Green Curry

Inter Restaurant , Bangkok

"Inter Restaurant has become a celebrated destination for authentic green curry , known for its bold flavors and perfectly balanced spice. The restaurant’s signature dish is crafted with the freshest Thai basil, kaffir lime leaves, and house-made green curry paste, creating a vibrant, aromatic experience that’s simply unmatched. Each bowl is simmered with tender cuts of chicken or shrimp, infused with coconut milk for a creamy, slightly sweet undertone that balances the dish’s heat. Served with jasmine rice, every spoonful captures the heart of Thailand’s culinary heritage. Thai Harmony’s green curry is a true masterpiece, earning its place as one of the best in the world.

Moules frites

Chez Leon restaurant , Belgium

Chez Leon restaurant is a must-visit for seafood lovers, offering a moules-frites experience that transports diners straight to the Belgian coast. Their mussels are sourced daily for the freshest flavor, steamed to perfection with white wine, garlic, and herbs that create an aromatic, flavorful broth. Served alongside crispy, golden frites that are double-fried for the perfect crunch, this dish is a harmony of textures and tastes. Each bite of the mussels, soaked in the rich, savory broth, paired with a dip of the frites, makes Le Mariner’s moules-frites an unforgettable culinary delight that has earned a global following.

Oyakadon

Tsukiji Toritoh, Japan

"Tsukiji Toritoh has mastered the art of oyakodon, offering a soulful bowl of comfort that’s revered by locals and visitors alike. Known for its simplicity and depth of flavor, Tokyo Bowl’s oyakodon features tender chicken simmered with sweet onions in a delicate dashi broth, then topped with perfectly set eggs for a creamy texture. Served over fluffy, steaming rice, each mouthful is a comforting blend of savory, slightly sweet, and umami flavors that capture the heart of Japanese home cooking. This restaurant’s commitment to tradition and quality ingredients makes their oyakodon a standout dish that’s widely regarded as the best in the world.
